About Me

My Dream Career

Hello! I’m Nurul Afyqah, and I'm currently a student in new media communication at the University of Malaysia Perlis. Ever since I began my studies, I have been deeply fascinated by how creativity, technology, and storytelling come together to create powerful messages that inspire and connect with people. My passion lies in visual design, multimedia, and branding, and I am eager to build a strong foundation in these areas through my academic journey. My dream is to become a Creative Director — someone who not only brings innovative ideas to life but also leads creative teams to produce meaningful and impactful work. I believe that with the right combination of skills, creativity, and leadership, I can help shape projects that captivate audiences and make a lasting impression. As a student, I am dedicated to learning as much as I can, from both my coursework and practical experiences. I actively seek opportunities to grow my skills in design software, content creation, and strategic thinking. I also enjoy collaborating with others, as I know teamwork is essential in the creative industry. Through this website and my journey, I hope to share my progress, inspirations, and the lessons I learn along the way. I am excited about the path ahead and committed to turning my passion into a rewarding career as a Creative Director.
